The ranked candidates

Seventy papers seeded so far; the forty ranked below are charted and filterable. Every score is earned against a fixed rubric — recency (25) + real-hardware evidence (25) + zero-shot status (20) + venue and citation velocity (15) + platform relevance (15) — and the breakdown is shown per paper, so no ranking is taken on trust.

Because 2025-26 papers cannot yet show citation velocity, lab pedigree, competition wins, hardware evidence and code releases carry that weight instead. Sources marked to confirm came from model knowledge and must be existence-checked before acquisition; the full 70-entry manifest with download instructions is docs/corpus-acquisition.md. Papers with an annotated page are linked directly.
